Week 1: Introduction to Security and Risk Management

Reflecting on Unit 1, I've gained a solid understanding of the critical elements in security and risk management. The unit clarified how essential it is to distinguish between risks, threats, and vulnerabilities within information systems. I now appreciate the complexity of the risk management process, particularly the importance of accurately assessing and mitigating risks. Learning about various methods to quantify and qualify risks, as well as how to select suitable risk standards, was particularly valuable. These insights will be crucial as I apply these principles in real-world scenarios, ensuring effective protection of information systems....
