Modules Covered:

    Project : [Unit Title]

    1. Collaborative Discussion Forum Summaries

    • Objective: Reflect on collaborative discussions that address learning outcomes, particularly in identifying and analysing security risks and ethical considerations.

    • Content: Summaries of key insights and conclusions drawn from forum discussions, highlighting how they contribute to understanding and addressing security risks.

    2. Outcomes from Team Exercises

    • Objective: Showcase team-based work focused on practical applications of the module's learning outcomes.

    • Content: Documentation of the exercises, including problem-solving approaches, risk assessment strategies, and collaborative decision-making processes.

    3. Reflective Piece

    • Objective: Provide a personal reflection on how the module has enhanced your understanding and skills in security risk management.

    • Content: Discuss your growth in critical thinking, problem-solving, and application of security risk management principles. Include specific examples from the module's activities.

    4. Summary of Learning Outcomes

    • Objective: Offer a concise summary of the key learning outcomes achieved throughout the module.

    • Content: Highlight your understanding of the main concepts and how you applied them in practical scenarios.

    5. Team Meeting Notes

    • Objective: Document the collaborative process and decision-making during team exercises.

    • Content: Detailed notes from team meetings, including discussions on risk analysis, methodologies used, and outcomes achieved.

    6. Feedback from Peers and Tutors

    • Objective: Incorporate constructive feedback to demonstrate ongoing professional development.

    • Content: Summaries of feedback received and reflections on how it has informed your learning and practice.

    7. Professional Skills Matrix and Action Plan (PDP)

    • Objective: Develop a personal development plan based on the skills gained in the module.

    • Content: A matrix outlining your strengths and areas for improvement, along with a detailed action plan for further skill development.

    8. Additional Artefacts

    • Objective: Include other relevant work or projects that demonstrate your competence in security risk management.

    • Content: Examples of completed assignments, case studies, or projects with explanations of how they relate to the module's learning outcomes.
